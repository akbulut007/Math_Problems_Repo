# Task 8 — Card Probabilities

## One card

Heart:

P = 13/52 = 1/4

King:

P = 4/52 = 1/13

Not a face card:

P = 40/52 = 10/13

---

## Two cards (with replacement)

Both hearts:

P = 1/4 × 1/4 = 1/16

Same rank:

P = 1/13

At least one ace:

P = 25/169

---

## Two cards (without replacement)

Both hearts:

P = 1/17

Same rank:

P = 1/17

One king and one queen:

P = 8/663
