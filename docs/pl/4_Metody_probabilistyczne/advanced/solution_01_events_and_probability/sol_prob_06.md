# Task 6 — Coin Toss Probabilities

## One toss

Heads:

P = 1/2

Tails:

P = 1/2

---

## Two tosses

Exactly one head:

HT, TH

P = 2/4 = 1/2

At least one head:

HH, HT, TH

P = 3/4

Same result:

HH, TT

P = 2/4 = 1/2

---

## Three tosses

Exactly two heads:

HHT, HTH, THH

P = 3/8

At least one tail:

All outcomes except HHH

P = 7/8

Same result:

HHH, TTT

P = 2/8 = 1/4
