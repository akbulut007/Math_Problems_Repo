# Task 10 — Buffon's Needle

Needle length = L  
Distance between lines = d

---

Needle crosses a line:

P = 2L / (dπ)

---

Needle does not cross a line:

P = 1 − 2L / (dπ)

---

Angle smaller than π/6:

P = 1/3

---

Distance from line < d/4:

P = 1/2

---

Crosses line and angle > π/4:

P = (L√2) / (dπ)
